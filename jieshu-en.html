Kessoku Band: Shimokitazawa Field Notes

[IKUYO NEWS Archive] Kessoku Band is a four-piece band active at Shimokitazawa STARRY. Their shows are known for youth, nerves, explosive energy, and occasional financial emergencies.

IKUYO NEWS tracks member updates, live rumors, music works, and unusual sightings around Shimokitazawa through our proudly over-serious newsroom lens.

Vocalist and guitarist. Her social energy level is exceptionally high.

Hitori Gotoh

Guitarist. Frequently enters emergency avoidance mode when the stage gets too bright.

Ryo Yamada

Bassist. Gifted, free-spirited, and reliably short on money.

Nijika Ijichi

Drummer. Keeps the rhythm, the schedule, and sometimes the entire band upright.
