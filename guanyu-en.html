About IKUYO NEWS

IKUYO NEWS is an in-universe fan news site centered on Ikuyo Kita, Kessoku Band, and Shimokitazawa rumors.

The site wraps fan jokes in the shape of serious news reporting. It looks official, but the sparkle level is intentionally high.

If you feel your eyes being purified while reading, lower your screen brightness and visit the Music section for more Kita energy.
