Breaking: STARRY audience asked to wear sunglasses after Kita Beam overload

Published: December 29, 2025

[Shimokitazawa] STARRY management issued an unusual safety notice this morning. Audience members attending Kessoku Band shows are now advised to bring anti-glare sunglasses.

The measure follows last night's incident, when vocalist and guitarist Ikuyo Kita reportedly produced a powerful positive aura during the performance.

A sacred panic spread through the venue.

One anonymous pink-tracksuit witness said the light was so bright that they had to hide behind nearby equipment. STARRY staff are now discussing brightness limits and a dedicated Kita Beam warning lamp.

Kita herself is reportedly trying to reduce her smile brightness, but early results remain limited.
